Who We Are

Your Comprehensive Design to Deliver Manufacturing Partner

We challenge the status quo in the kitchen appliance industry by prioritizing design and innovation for leading brands.

Isler is the country's first design-focused solutions company specializing in kitchen appliance manufacturing for various brands. Our diverse product portfolio includes kitchen chimneys, built-in hobs, cooktops, as well as tablets, OTGs, microwaves, and dishwashers.

Equipped with in-house design, R&D team, state-of-the-art-factory, and strong global partnerships, we expedite new product development. We focus on localizing essential components to foster innovation and streamline the development process, utilizing our expertise in forging, casting, machining, rolling, extrusion, and fabrication. By leveraging our advanced technology, we provide transparency and reliability to our clients through Supply Network Control Tower (SNCT) platform.
